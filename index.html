Why Consider ICHRA?

Five reasons ICHRA is the fastest-growing health benefit in America

01

Predictable, Fixed Costs

You set a fixed monthly allowance per employee. No more surprise premium increases at renewal based on claims experience.

02

Employee Choice & Flexibility

Employees choose from dozens of marketplace plans that fit their doctors, needs, and budget — not one carrier's limited network.

03

Fully ACA Compliant

ICHRA is an official IRS regulation (established 2020) satisfying ACA employer mandate requirements when properly structured.

04

No Minimums — No Participation Requirements

Unlike group plans, ICHRA has zero minimum contribution amounts and no employee participation thresholds. Any size business qualifies.

05

Simplified Administration

No carrier negotiations, no network management. Approve monthly reimbursements through a simple admin platform.
