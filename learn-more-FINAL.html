Why Consider ICHRA?

Five key benefits that make ICHRA worth exploring

💰

Predictable, Fixed Costs

You set a fixed monthly allowance per employee. No more surprise premium increases at renewal based on claims experience.

🎯

Employee Choice & Flexibility

Employees choose from dozens of marketplace plans that fit their needs, doctors, and budget — not limited to one carrier's network.

⚖️

Fully ACA Compliant

ICHRA is an official IRS regulation (established 2020) that satisfies ACA employer mandate requirements when properly structured.

🔧

Customizable by Employee Class

Offer different allowances for full-time vs part-time, salaried vs hourly, or by location — flexibility traditional plans don't allow.

📉

Simplified Administration

No carrier negotiations, participation requirements, or network management. Just approve monthly reimbursements through an admin platform.
